Swipeology Privacy Policy (Copy & Paste This) Last Updated: February 2026 Swipeology (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Swipeology mobile application (“App”). By using Swipeology, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect 1.1 Information You Provide We may collect the following information when you create an account or use the App: Name Email address School or campus affiliation Profile details (bio, photos, interests, major, class year, pronouns) Messages you send to matches Preferences (such as dating preferences or friend‑only mode) 1.2 Automatically Collected Information We may automatically collect: Device information (model, OS version) App usage data IP address Crash logs and performance data 1.3 Location Information Swipeology may request access to your approximate location to help show relevant profiles at your school or campus. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Create and manage your Swipeology account Display your profile to other users Match you with other users based on your preferences Enable messaging between matched users Improve app performance and user experience Maintain safety and prevent fraud Comply with legal requirements We do not sell your personal information. 3. How We Share Your Information We may share your information with: Service providers (hosting, analytics, authentication) Other users, but only the information you choose to include in your profile Law enforcement, if required by law or to protect user safety We do not share your personal data with advertisers or third‑party marketing companies. 4. Data Security We use industry‑standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 5. Your Choices You can: Edit your profile at any time Delete your account Disable location access Request deletion of your data To request data deletion, contact us at: support@swipeology.org 6. Children’s Privacy Swipeology is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. 7.
