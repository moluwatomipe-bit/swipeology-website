Swipeology Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Data Deletion Policy explains how Swipeology handles user data when a deletion request is made. We are committed to respecting your privacy and giving you control over your information.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data in two ways:

  • By deleting your account directly in the Swipeology app
  • By emailing us at swipeologyapp@gmail.com

2. What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request deletion, we remove or anonymize the following:

  • Your account information
  • Your profile details
  • Your photos
  • Your matches
  • Your messages (deleted or anonymized)

Most data is removed from active systems within 30 days. Some information may remain in secure backups for a limited period as required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

3. Verification

For security, we may verify your identity before processing a deletion request.

4. Irreversible Action

Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you return to Swipeology.

5. Contact

If you have questions about data deletion or need help, contact us at:

swipeologyapp@gmail.com
