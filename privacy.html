Swipeology Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Swipeology (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Swipeology mobile application (“App”). By using Swipeology, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • School or campus affiliation
  • Profile details (bio, photos, interests, major, class year, pronouns)
  • Messages you send to matches
  • Preferences (dating preferences or friend‑only mode)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data
  • IP address
  • Crash logs and performance data

1.3 Location Information

Swipeology may request access to your approximate location to help show relevant profiles at your school or campus. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your Swipeology account
  • Display your profile to other users
  • Match you with other users based on your preferences
  • Enable messaging between matched users
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Maintain safety and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, authentication)
  • Other users (only the information you choose to include in your profile)
  • Law enforcement, if required by law or to protect user safety

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or third‑party marketing companies.

4. Data Security

We use industry‑standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

  • Edit your profile at any time
  • Delete your account
  • Disable location access
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing swipeologyapp@gmail.com

6. Children’s Privacy

Swipeology is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent version.
