Swipeology Safety Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Swipeology is committed to creating a respectful, safe, and inclusive environment for all users. This Safety Policy explains the standards and expectations for behavior within the Swipeology app.

1. Respectful Behavior

The following behavior is strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sexual harassment or unsolicited explicit content
  • Stalking, intimidation, or coercion

2. Profile Guidelines

Your profile must not include:

  • Nudity or sexually explicit images
  • Violent, graphic, or disturbing content
  • False or misleading identity information
  • Personal contact information (phone number, address, etc.)

3. Messaging Guidelines

Do not send:

  • Unwanted sexual messages
  • Spam or scam attempts
  • Requests for money or financial help
  • Abusive, threatening, or degrading language

4. Reporting & Blocking

Users may report or block others at any time. Reports are reviewed to maintain safety and enforce community standards.

5. Real‑World Safety

When meeting someone from Swipeology, we recommend:

  • Meet in public places
  • Tell a friend where you’re going
  • Keep personal information private
  • Trust your instincts and leave if you feel uncomfortable

6. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • A warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account removal

7. Contact
